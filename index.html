WEBSITE PURPOSE

THIS WEBSITE IS CREATED BY BSBA FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT STUDENTS FROM BATANGAS STATE UNIVERSITY – THE NATIONAL ENGINEERING UNIVERSITY (PB MAIN 1).

IT AIMS TO EMPOWER WORKING STUDENTS BY PROVIDING TOOLS, STRATEGIES, AND MOTIVATIONAL RESOURCES TO ENHANCE THEIR PERCEIVED SOCIO-ECONOMIC STATUS (PSES) AND INCREASE MONTHLY INCOME – TWO OF THE LOWEST-SCORING ASPECTS OF FINANCIAL WELL-BEING BASED ON OUR STUDY.

THE CONTENTS COMBINE RESEARCH FINDINGS, REAL-LIFE EXAMPLES, AND MULTIMEDIA LEARNING TO MAKE FINANCIAL LITERACY PRACTICAL AND RELATABLE.

INTRODUCTION

WELCOME TO EMPOWERFIN, A PLATFORM BUILT TO SUPPORT AND INSPIRE WORKING STUDENTS AT BATANGAS STATE UNIVERSITY – TNEU PB MAIN 1.

OUR RESEARCH, "EFFECTS OF FINANCIAL LITERACY AND FINANCIAL STRESS ON FINANCIAL WELL-BEING WITH FINANCIAL BEHAVIOR AS MEDIATOR", REVEALED THAT MONTHLY INCOME AND PERCEIVED SOCIO-ECONOMIC STATUS SIGNIFICANTLY AFFECT FINANCIAL WELL-BEING.

THIS WEBSITE TRANSFORMS THOSE INSIGHTS INTO PRACTICAL TOOLS AND EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS.

MISSION STATEMENT

"TO UPLIFT THE FINANCIAL CAPACITY AND SELF-PERCEIVED STATUS OF WORKING STUDENTS THROUGH FINANCIAL EDUCATION, EMPOWERMENT, AND COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT."

VISION

A FINANCIALLY LITERATE, CONFIDENT, AND RESILIENT BATSTATEU STUDENT COMMUNITY EQUIPPED WITH LIFE-LONG MONEY MANAGEMENT SKILLS.

WHAT IS FINANCIAL WELL-BEING?

FINANCIAL WELL-BEING REFERS TO FEELING SECURE AND IN CONTROL OF YOUR MONEY – BEING ABLE TO MEET CURRENT AND FUTURE OBLIGATIONS COMFORTABLY.

(SOURCE: CFPB, 2022)

KEY CONCEPTS

FINANCIAL LITERACY

UNDERSTANDING FINANCIAL TERMS, BUDGETING, SAVING, INVESTING.

EXAMPLE: KNOWING HOW TO USE A BANK APP OR BUDGET WORKSHEET.

FINANCIAL BEHAVIOR

DAY-TO-DAY HABITS THAT AFFECT FINANCES.

EXAMPLE: TRACKING EXPENSES, SAVING REGULARLY.

FINANCIAL STRESS

EMOTIONAL TENSION FROM MONEY-RELATED PROBLEMS.

EXAMPLE: WORRYING ABOUT PAYING TUITION OR RENT.

FINANCIAL WELL-BEING

LEVEL OF SATISFACTION AND CONFIDENCE IN YOUR FINANCIAL LIFE.

EXAMPLE: BEING ABLE TO PAY BILLS AND STILL SAVE.

— SHIM, BARBER & SERIDO, 2010
